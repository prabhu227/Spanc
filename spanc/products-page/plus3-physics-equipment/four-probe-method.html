Technical Specifications

  • Voltmeter: 3½ digit, 7-segment LED, auto polarity & decimal indication
  • Voltage Range: X1 (0–200.0mV DC), X10 (0–2.00V DC), 4mm socket
  • Ammeter: 3 digit, 7-segment LED, Range: 0–20mA DC, 4mm socket
  • Oven Supply: 45V AC (LOW), 60V AC (HIGH)
  • Oven Connector: 5-Pin DIN type, 220V AC input, Fuse: 1A / 250V
  • Probes: Spring type, spacing 25mm
  • Crystal: Ge (P-type), Size: 12x14x0.5 mm, Orientation: <100>
  • Resistivity: 1–10 ohm-cm
  • Thermometer: Mercury, Range: 0–200°C, Least Count: 1°C, Length: 300mm

Experiment

To measure the resistivity of a semiconductor (Germanium) crystal as a function of temperature using the Four-Probe Method.

Scope of Supply

  • Four Probe Setup
  • Germanium Crystal
  • Current Source and Measurement System
  • Oven
  • Thermometer
  • Instruction Manual

Note

The plot of resistivity vs. temperature data can be used to determine the energy band gap of the Germanium sample.
