RAYLEIGH’S BRIDGE METHOD

Product Specifications

  • Definition Ballistic galvanometer
  • Moving coil should be free from magnetic material
  • Suspension is non-conducting
  • The terminal, coil, and other connections should be of copper to avoid thermo-electric effects
  • Lamp and scale arrangement
  • Decade resistance (10x10Ω, 10x1Ω & 10x0.1Ω)
  • Fixed value low resistance (0.01Ω to 0.17Ω)
  • Fixed standard resistance (1Ω, 10Ω, 100Ω & 1000Ω)
  • Unknown Inductance
