Educational Note

Young’s modulus is an index measuring the elastic property of a material. Elasticity refers to a material’s ability to resist deformation under applied stress and to return to its original shape. The restoring force is an internal response that opposes deformation. Young’s modulus is defined as the ratio of restoring stress to strain and is a key parameter in material mechanics.
