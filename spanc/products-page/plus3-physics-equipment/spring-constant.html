Spring Constant Apparatus

Model Number: SPML-009

Technical Specifications

  • Adjustable scale mounted on a 37.5 cm stand
  • Helical Spring: Diameter 25 mm, Length 10 cm
  • Attached pointer for displacement reading
  • Heavy Metal Base Plate for stability
  • Metal Rod for mounting
  • Weights: 5 pieces of 50 gm (total 250 gm)

Experiment

  • To study the motion of a spring and determine the Spring Constant (Hooke’s Law)

Scope of Supply

  • Spring Constant Apparatus
  • Set of Weights: 50g × 5
  • Instruction Manual
About the Experiment

This apparatus is designed to verify Hooke's Law: \( W = kx \), where k is the spring constant and x is the displacement. By measuring how far the spring stretches for various weights, students can plot a graph and determine the slope, which represents the spring constant.
