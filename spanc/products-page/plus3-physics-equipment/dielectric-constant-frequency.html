Frequency Dependence of Dielectric Constant

Experiment: To study the frequency dependence of dielectric constant and loss factor for various capacitor types.

Technical Specifications

  • Capacitance measurement from 1–50kHz
  • Dedicated Schering Bridge with built-in oscillator
  • Computation of dielectric constant and loss factor
  • Samples: Barium Titanate, MLCC, Conventional capacitor
  • Optional: Oven and controller for temperature studies
  • Essential: CRO required for experiments

Introduction

Barium Titanate (BaTiO₃) is a widely investigated dielectric material, commonly used in capacitors due to its high dielectric constant.

This experiment investigates how capacitance and dielectric losses vary with frequency and temperature for Barium Titanate and other types of capacitors.

Experiments

  • Loss factor vs frequency for Barium Titanate
  • Capacitance vs frequency for MLCC and ceramic capacitors
  • Dielectric constant variation with temperature (optional)
