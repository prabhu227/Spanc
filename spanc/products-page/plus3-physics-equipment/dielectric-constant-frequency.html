Frequency Dependence of Electric Constant

The following experiment studies the frequency dependence of dielectric constant and loss factor for various capacitor types.

  • Capacitance measurement from 1-50kHz
  • Dedicated Schering Bridge with built-in oscillator
  • Computation of dielectric constant and loss factor
  • Samples: Barium Titanate, MLCC, Conventional capacitor
  • Optional equipment: Oven and controller for temperature studies
  • Essential accessory: CRO for all experiments
