About the Experiment

Poiseuille's method uses a capillary tube to measure the viscosity of a liquid by observing the rate of fluid flow through the tube under a given pressure difference. By measuring the flow rate and knowing the tube's dimensions, the coefficient of viscosity of the liquid can be determined.
