Gouy’s Method

Experimental setup to measure the magnetic susceptibility of solids using Gouy’s Method.

Technical Specifications

  • Electromagnet: Soft iron, 75mm dia. pole pieces. Magnetic Field: 20KG at 6mm gap. Coils: 13W each (approx.).
  • Digital Power Supply: 0–6A, 30V with digital display. Stable constant current for long operations.
  • Digital Gauss Meter: 0–2K & 0–20K Gauss ranges. Resolution: 1 Gauss. Accuracy: ±0.5%. 3½-digit LED display. 220V, 50Hz. Field direction indicator.
  • Digital Balance: 200g capacity, 0.001g least count, LCD back-lit display, external calibration, 80mm pan diameter.

Experiment

To measure the magnetic susceptibility of solid materials using Gouy’s Method. The force acting on the sample in a magnetic field is determined by precise mass changes.
