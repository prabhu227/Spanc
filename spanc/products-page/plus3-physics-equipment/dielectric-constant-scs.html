Introduction

Barium Titanate (BaTiO₃) is a widely studied dielectric material, used primarily in capacitors due to its high dielectric constant. Its dielectric properties are influenced by purity, microstructure, and preparation techniques. These properties are also sensitive to electric fields, temperature, and frequency, making it ideal for dielectric characterization experiments.

SSP-008 Experiment

This experiment focuses on determining the Hall coefficient of a semiconductor sample using an electromagnet and a digital Gauss meter to analyze the material’s magnetic response.
