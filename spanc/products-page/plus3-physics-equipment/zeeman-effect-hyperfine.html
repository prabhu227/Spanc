Study of Zeeman Effect

Model: SK - 075

Technical Specification

  • Power Supply: 0–30V DC, 10A; Input: 220V ±5%, 3½ digit LED display
  • Electromagnet: 400 turns coil, 10A max, U & I cores (150mm, 40×40mm)
  • Optical Bench: Aluminium alloy, hexagonal, 0–100cm scale, 1mm LC
  • Fabry Perot Etalon: λ/20 mirror optics, 3mm adjustable gap, 532nm green filter
  • CCD Camera: CMOS sensor, VGA output, BNC, manual focus, 5V DC
  • Polarizer Filter: 0°–90° adjustable, 21mm aperture, 130mm frame, linear
  • Quarter Wave Plate: 0°–90° adjustable, 15mm aperture, circular polarization

Experiment

To study the Zeeman effect in the presence of an external magnetic field and analyze hyperfine splitting.
