Dielectric Constant of a Dielectric Material

Experimental setup for determining the dielectric constant of materials using capacitive measurements.

Technical Specifications

  • Actual Capacitors: 18pF, 13pF, 10pF, 8pF
  • Test Capacitor without Dielectric: 80pF, 88pF, 91pF, 94pF
  • Test Capacitor with Dielectric: 25pF, 46pF, 60pF, 70pF
  • Current Meter
  • High Frequency Oscillator: 100KHz
  • Gang Condenser: 0° to 180°
  • Mounted on Hylem Board
  • 4mm Interconnection Terminals
  • Power Supply: 230V / 50Hz

Experiment

To determine the dielectric constant of various dielectric materials by comparing capacitor values with and without the dielectric substance inserted.

Scope of Supply

  • Trainer Kit
  • Dielectric Sample
  • Instruction Manual
