Explanation

The Solar Cell I-V Characteristic Curves show the current and voltage (I-V) characteristics of a particular photovoltaic (PV) cell, module, or array. It gives a detailed description of its solar energy conversion ability and efficiency. Understanding the electrical I-V characteristics, especially the maximum power point, is critical in determining the device's output performance and solar efficiency.

Photovoltaic solar cells convert the sun’s radiant light directly into electricity. With the increasing demand for clean energy and the sun’s potential as a free energy source, solar energy conversion is increasingly part of renewable energy solutions. As a result, the demand for efficient solar cells that directly convert sunlight into electricity is growing faster than ever before.
