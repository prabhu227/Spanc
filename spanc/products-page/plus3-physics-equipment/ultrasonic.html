Velocity of Ultrasonic Waves in a Liquid Apparatus

Experiment: To determine the wavelength and velocity of ultrasonic waves in a liquid (Kerosene Oil, Xylene, etc.) by studying the diffraction through ultrasonic grating.

Technical Specification

  • Spectrometer: Scale: SS, dia. 7inch, Vernier constant 30 second, provided with 20" long telescope, Magnification using 50mm dia acromatic objective lens.
  • Collimator Tube: 1" dia acromatic objective and a sharp adjustable slit, telescope adjusted for parallel rays with a cut mark for alignment.
  • Prism Table: Conventional replaced by a special rectangular table for placing the glass cell.
  • High Frequency Generator: Frequency range - 3MHz, RF crystal, Frequency variable pot, frequency scale. Liquid set into longitudinal vibrations using a crystal and RF oscillator.
