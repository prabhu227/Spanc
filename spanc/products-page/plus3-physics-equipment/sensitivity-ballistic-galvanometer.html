SENSITIVITY USING BALLISTIC GALVANOMETER

EME-002 Product Specifications

  • Definition Ballistic galvanometer
  • Moving coil should be free from magnetic material
  • Suspension is non-conducting
  • The terminal, coil and other connections should be of copper in order to avoid thermo-electric effects
  • Lamp and scale arrangement
  • Battery Eliminator: 2V DC/250mA
  • Resistance box: 10000Ω, 2110Ω/1110Ω each
  • One way key
  • Tapping key
  • Digital Stop watch
