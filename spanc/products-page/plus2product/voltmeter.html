The Voltmeter is a fundamental instrument used for measuring the electrical potential difference between two points in an electric circuit. Ideal for classroom demonstrations, laboratory experiments, and technical training setups, it offers clear readings with high accuracy. DC mounted in beautiful ABS-Plastic stand accuracy +2.0% F.S.D. with lock type terminals. All common ranges 3V, 5V, 10V available
