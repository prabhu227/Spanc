Parallelogram of Forces Apparatus

Product Code: 1405

The Parallelogram of Forces Apparatus is used to experimentally verify the parallelogram law of forces and determine the resultant of two concurrent forces. This classic apparatus is essential for teaching vector resolution and force equilibrium in physics labs.
