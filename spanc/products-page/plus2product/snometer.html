Sonometer Apparatus

Product Code: 1472

The Sonometer Apparatus is used for the study of the relationship between frequency and the tension, length, and mass per unit length of a vibrating string. This apparatus is ideal for classroom demonstrations and experiments in sound and wave physics.

Technical Specifications

  • Product Name: Sonometer Apparatus
  • Product Code: 1472
  • Material: Polished wooden base, metal pulley, wire, movable bridges
  • Function: Study of frequency relation with length, tension, and linear density
  • Construction: Rectangular wooden box with graduated scale and fixed/movable bridges
  • Application: To verify the laws of vibrating strings and determine unknown frequencies
