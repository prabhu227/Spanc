Meter Bridge

Product Code: 1056

The Meter Bridge (Wheatstone Bridge) is one meter long and features four gaps/two gaps, a plywood base, a sunmica top, a graduated scale, and brass C.P. strips. It is designed for measuring unknown resistances in electrical circuits and is mounted on a smooth wooden base for easy handling. The bridge is ideal for use in conjunction with a galvanometer and a known resistance to perform accurate resistance measurements.
