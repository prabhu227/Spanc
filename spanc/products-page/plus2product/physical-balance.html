Physical Balance

Product Code: 1155

The Physical Balance is a fundamental laboratory instrument used for comparing the masses of objects with high accuracy. Suitable for physics and chemistry labs, it is designed for precision and durability, making it ideal for educational experiments. With three stone agate, capacity 250gm. Sensitivity 5mg. On wooden base with leveling screws, fitted in glass showcase. The balance is provided with a set of weights: 1gm, 2gm, 5gm, 10gm, 20gm, 50gm, 100gm, and 200gm.
