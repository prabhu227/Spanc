Transistor Characteristic Apparatus

Product Code: 1269

The Transistor Characteristic Apparatus is designed to study the input and output characteristics of NPN and PNP transistors in Common Base and Common Emitter configurations. Equipped with a built-in regulated power supply, it includes four analog meters (3V, 250μA, 50mA, 10V) and is ideal for demonstrating transistor behavior and amplification in physics labs.

Technical Specifications

  • Product Name: Transistor Characteristic Apparatus
  • Product Code: 1269
  • Transistor Type: NPN and PNP (switchable)
  • Modes Supported: Common Emitter and Common Base
  • Power Supply: Built-in regulated DC supply
  • Indicators: Analog voltmeter and ammeter for voltage and current readings

Additional Details

  • Included Meters: MO 65 moving coil meters (3V, 250μA, 50mA, 10V)
  • Transistor Mounting: Front panel mounted AC128 PNP transistor
  • Terminals: Color-coded banana sockets for connections
  • Educational Level: High School, Diploma, and Undergraduate Physics Labs

Applications and Experiment Usage

  • Study input/output characteristics of transistors in CE/CB modes
  • Understand amplification and transistor operating principles
  • Ideal for analog electronics and solid-state device experiments
