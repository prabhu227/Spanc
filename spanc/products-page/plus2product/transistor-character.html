Transistor Characteristic Apparatus

Product Code: 1269

The Transistor Characteristic Apparatus is designed to study input and output characteristics of NPN and PNP transistors in both Common Base and Common Emitter configurations. It features a built-in dual stabilized continuously variable DC power supply and clearly labeled terminals for easy connection. With analog meters and a PNP AC128 transistor mounted on the front panel, this apparatus helps students understand transistor principles, characteristics, and amplification. Suitable for high school, diploma, and undergraduate physics labs.

Technical Specifications

  • Product Name: Transistor Characteristic Apparatus
  • Product Code: 1269
  • Transistor Type: NPN and PNP (switchable configuration)
  • Modes Supported: Common Emitter and Common Base
  • Power Supply: Built-in regulated DC supply
  • Indicators: Analog voltmeter and ammeter for readings
  • Front Panel: Mounted with AC128 PNP transistor and connection terminals

Additional Details

  • Educational Level: High School, Diploma, and Undergraduate Labs
  • Terminals: Color-coded banana sockets for easy and secure connections
  • Learning Outcome: Understanding working principles, characteristics, and amplification of transistors
  • Applications: Ideal for classroom demonstrations and student lab experiments
