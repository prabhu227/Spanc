Leclanché Cell Electronic

Product Code: 1144

The Leclanché Cell Electronic model demonstrates the principles of electrochemical reactions and battery operation. It consists of carbon and zinc electrodes immersed in an electrolyte solution sealed in a durable body, ideal for academic experiments.

Technical Specifications

  • Electrodes: Zinc (anode) and Carbon rod (cathode)
  • Electrolyte: Ammonium chloride solution with manganese dioxide
  • Body: Sealed plastic or glass container for safety and visibility
  • Terminals: Clearly marked for external connection

Application

  • Used to demonstrate chemical-to-electrical energy conversion

Educational Scope

  • Educational Level: Suitable for physics, chemistry, and electrochemistry labs
  • Learning Outcome: Understand the principles of electrochemical reactions and battery operation.

Experiment Usage

  • Ideal for experiments on electrolysis and battery functioning in academic labs.
