Zener Diode Characteristic Apparatus

Product Code: 1268

The Zener Diode Characteristic Apparatus is designed with a built-in stabilized power supply and uses two MO 65 moving coil meters for voltage and current measurements. The apparatus allows students to study the V-I characteristics of the Zener diode in forward and reverse bias conditions.

Technical Specifications

  • Product Name: Zener Diode Characteristic Apparatus
  • Product Code: 1268
  • Function: To study V-I characteristics of Zener diode in forward and reverse bias
  • Breakdown Analysis: Shows clear breakdown region and voltage regulation
  • Included: Zener diode setup, voltmeter, ammeter, and regulated power supply
  • Application: Ideal for learning diode breakdown and voltage control

Additional Details

  • Educational Level: School, Diploma, and Undergraduate Electronics Labs
  • Mounting: Banana socket terminals for easy wiring and measurement
  • Learning Outcome: Understanding of voltage regulation and Zener effect in electronics
