Triode Valve Characteristic Apparatus

Product Code: 1265

The Triode Valve Characteristic Apparatus comes with a built-in stabilized power supply, designed to operate on 230V AC. The unit features a triode valve mounted on the front panel and is equipped with two MO 65 moving coil meters (one dual range). It provides a high voltage output (0–300V DC at 30mA), a bias supply (0–15V DC at 5mA), and low voltage AC (3V at 3A), making it ideal for plotting anode and mutual characteristics of vacuum tubes in electronics labs.
