Rising Table (Slow Motion Stand)

Product Code: 1449

A machine cast aluminium table 10cm dia having a stem of 12cm can be lowered or raised to desired height and it can be tightened with a thumb screw. In addition to this coarse movement, fine adjustment is provided by a flush in arrangement fitted on a heavy circular base with two leveling screws and one rod to hold the capillary clamp, supplied with capillary clamp.
