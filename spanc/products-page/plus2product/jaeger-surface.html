Jaeger's Surface Tension Apparatus

Product Code: 1450

This method is suitable for the purpose of studying the variation of surface tension with concentration of solution of common salt in water.

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Glass bulb, capillary, and metallic stand with adjustable holder
  • Principle: Measures pressure at which air bubble forms at the tip of the capillary submerged in liquid
  • Components: Jaeger’s bulb, fine capillary, manometer, beaker stand, and support rod
  • Application: To determine the surface tension of various liquids in laboratory experiments

Educational Scope

  • Educational Level: High School, College & University Physics Labs
  • Learning Outcome: Understand the principles of surface tension and its variation with concentration in liquids.

Application

  • Used for measuring surface tension in laboratory experiments involving various liquids.
