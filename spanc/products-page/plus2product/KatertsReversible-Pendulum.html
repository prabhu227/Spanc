Kater's Reversible Pendulum

Product Code: 1393

Consists of steel C.P. rod one meter long, two adjustable knife edges facing each other, two cast iron weights are provided to slide along the length of the bar and can be clamped in any position. The apparatus is used to find acceleration due to gravity at a place.

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Stainless Steel / Brass
  • Length of Pendulum: Approx. 100 cm
  • Features: Adjustable knife edges, precise pivot points, high accuracy
  • Application: Measuring acceleration due to gravity, Time period calculation

Included Items

  • Reversible Pendulum Rod
  • Two Adjustable Knife Edges
  • Slotted Weights
  • Support Stand

Educational Scope

  • Educational Level: Suitable for +2 level, undergraduate physics labs
  • Learning Outcome: Determine acceleration due to gravity, understanding pendulum dynamics.

Experiment Usage
