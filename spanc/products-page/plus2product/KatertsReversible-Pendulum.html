Consists of steel C.P. rod one meter long, two adjustable knife edges facing each other, two cast iron weights are provided to slide along the length of the bar and can be clamped in any position, two hardwood weights can be used to compensate iron weights. The apparatus is used to find acceleration due to gravity at a place.
