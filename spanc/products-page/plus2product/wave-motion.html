Wave Motion Apparatus

Product Code: 1477

The Wave Motion Apparatus is designed to demonstrate transverse wave motion using rods mounted on a rotating base. When the handle is turned, motion is transferred from one rod to the next, appearing as a traveling wave. This apparatus is perfect for teaching wave mechanics and oscillations in physics labs.

  • Product Code: 1477
  • Material: Metal frame, steel rods, wooden or metal base
  • Function: Demonstrates transverse wave propagation through rod displacement
  • Design: Equally spaced rods mounted on rotating horizontal base with damping end
  • Application: Used in classrooms for wave mechanics and oscillation demonstrations
