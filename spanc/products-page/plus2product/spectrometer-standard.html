Spectrometer Standard

Product Code: 1325

A basic instrument for performing spectroscopy experiments, designed to give the best possible results, mounted on a stable cast iron base. The protracted scale of 150mm is divided on a precision machine with a vernier reading of 1 minute. The telescope is fitted with a 25mm diameter and 178mm focal length achromatic objective, 10x Ramsden eyepiece, and cross-line graticule. The collimeter has an adjustable precision slit of 6mm, with non-rusting metal jaws, and the 8cm diameter prism/grating table is adjustable and has an engraved ring pattern. Supplied in a beautiful plywood box with prism and grating holders.
