Ohm's Law Apparatus

Product Code: 1258

The Ohm's Law Apparatus helps verify the relationship between voltage, current, and resistance (V = IR). It is equipped with a step-down transformer that provides output voltages of 1.5V, 3V, 4.5V, and 6V. This apparatus is ideal for physics experiments and educational purposes.
