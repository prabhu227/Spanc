Young's Modulus Apparatus (Searle's Type)

Product Code: 1395

The Young's Modulus Apparatus (Searle's Type) is used to determine Young’s Modulus by measuring wire elongation under varying loads. It includes a micrometer screw gauge, a twin frame setup, and precision weights.

  • Micrometer Screw Gauge: 0.01 mm least count for precision measurements
  • Setup: Twin-frame configuration for reference and test wire
  • Application: To calculate Young's modulus using extension under load
