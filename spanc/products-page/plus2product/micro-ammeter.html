Micro-Ammeter

Product Code: 1225

The Micro-Ammeter is a precise analog instrument designed to measure very small electric currents, typically in microamperes (μA). Ideal for experiments requiring sensitive current detection, such as in semiconductor, transistor, and electrolysis experiments in academic laboratories.

Technical Specifications

  • Product Name: Micro-Ammeter
  • Product Code: 1225
  • Measuring Range: 0–50 μA / 0–100 μA / 0–500 μA (varies by model)
  • Display Type: Analog needle dial
  • Sensitivity: High sensitivity for detecting micro-level currents
  • Application: Used in electronic and physics experiments for current measurement

Additional Details

  • Mount Type: Benchtop with screw terminals
  • Material: Metal body with plastic insulation, glass front
  • Educational Level: High School, College, and Electronics Labs

Applications and Experiment Usage

  • Ideal for use in semiconductor, transistor, and electrolysis experiments in academic labs.
  • Perfect for measuring tiny currents in electronic circuits and components.
