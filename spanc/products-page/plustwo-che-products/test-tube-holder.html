Test Tube Holder

A versatile tool for securely holding test tubes during laboratory experiments.

The Test Tube Holder is an essential tool for laboratories, providing a safe and secure way to hold test tubes during various experiments. It is available in different materials and designs to suit the needs of any lab.

Technical Specifications

  • Material Options: Iron, Brass, Stainless Steel
  • Design: Flat or Cross Pattern
  • Capacity: 3 to 8 test tubes, depending on model
  • Dimensions: Varies with model
  • Uses: Holding test tubes securely during heating, cooling, or transfer
  • Finish: C.P. (Copper Plated) or Stainless Steel
