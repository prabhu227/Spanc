Digital Melting Point Apparatus (Al-122)

Product Code: Al-122

The Digital Melting Point Apparatus (Al-122) is a precise laboratory tool used to determine the melting points of various substances. It features a 4-digit LED display and offers a temperature range from ambient to 275°C, providing reliable results for educational and research purposes.

Technical Specifications

  • Product Code: Al-122
  • Display: 4 Digit Seven Segment LED
  • Temperature Range: Ambient to 275°C
  • Resolution: 1°C
  • Capillary Glass Tube: 100mm Long, 0.8 to 1.1 mm Internal Diameter
  • Oil Bath: Silicon Oil Bath Beaker-200 ml Capacity
  • Power: 220 V ±10% AC, 50 Hz
  • Accessories: Oil Immersion Heater, Capillaries, Thermometer, Operating Manual

Additional Details

  • Educational Level: Ideal for high school and college students
  • Learning Outcome: Understanding of melting point determination and lab procedures

Applications and Experiment Usage

  • Used to determine the melting point of substances in laboratory settings.
  • Ideal for chemistry students and researchers in academic labs.
