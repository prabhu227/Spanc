Litmus Paper Red / Blue

Acid-Base Indicator for Laboratory Use

Litmus Paper (Red / Blue) is an essential pH indicator tool used for determining the acidity or alkalinity of a solution. These papers are widely employed in school laboratories, colleges, and industrial testing applications.

Technical Specifications

  • Type: Litmus Indicator Paper
  • Color: Available in Red and Blue variants
  • Form: Leaf Books
  • Quantity: Each packet contains 10 books
  • Leaves per Book: 50 leaves
  • Storage: Store in a dry and cool place

Applications

  • Testing the acidity or alkalinity of solutions
  • Ideal for chemistry experiments in school and college labs
  • Used in industrial environments for basic pH screening
