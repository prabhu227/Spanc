Silica Crucible

Durable high-temperature resistant crucible ideal for chemical and thermal analysis in laboratories

The Silica Crucible is designed to withstand high temperatures, making it perfect for chemical and thermal analysis in laboratory settings. With its durable construction, it ensures safe and accurate heating of substances.
