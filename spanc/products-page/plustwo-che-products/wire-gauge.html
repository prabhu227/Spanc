Wire Gauge

Durable gauze for heating flasks and beakers safely over Bunsen burners

The Wire Gauge is essential in laboratories, designed to evenly distribute heat under flasks and beakers when used over a Bunsen burner or tripod stand. Available in asbestos and full-metal frame variants, it ensures safety and durability during laboratory heating operations.

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Iron mesh with asbestos coating or full metal frame
  • Variants:
    • No. 1: 6" x 6" with asbestos center
    • No. 2: 6" x 6" with full metal frame (Superior quality)
  • Purpose: Distributes heat evenly under flasks and beakers
  • Application: Commonly used with Tripod Stands or Ring Clamps in chemistry labs
