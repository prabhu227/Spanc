Hot Air Oven (HCS-101)

Product Code: HCS-101

The Hot Air Oven (HCS-101) is ideal for sterilization and drying applications in laboratories. Designed with precision heating technology and durable construction, it provides consistent and accurate temperature control across a range of sizes.

Technical Specifications

  • Capacity: A-28L, B-45L, C-65L, D-95L, E-125L, F-224L, G-252L, H-336L, I-720L
  • Chamber Size: A-300×300×300 mm (12″×12″×12″), B-350×350×350 mm (14″×14″×14″), and so on
  • Shelves: 2 per model
  • Heating Elements: 3-sided heating

Applications

  • Used for drying glassware and sterilizing lab equipment
  • Maintains stable temperature for chemical processes requiring dry heat
  • Suitable for microbiology, medical, pharmaceutical, and research labs
