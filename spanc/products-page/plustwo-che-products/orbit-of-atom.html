Orbit of Atom Model

Educational tool to visualize atomic orbitals and electron configurations

The Orbit of Atom Model is a hands-on educational resource designed to demonstrate the atomic structure and the concept of electron orbitals. It provides a visual and physical representation of how electrons move around the nucleus in different energy levels.

Technical Specifications

  • Product Code: 2110
  • Model Type: Orbit of Atom (Electron Orbital Model)
  • Material: Durable acrylic or plastic
  • Size: Approx. 10–15 inches in diameter
  • Orbitals: 3–4 color-coded orbitals
  • Electron Indicators: Includes small balls or markers to represent electrons

Educational Applications

  • Ideal for classroom demonstrations of atomic theory
  • Helps students understand electron shells and energy levels
  • Used in science workshops and chemistry teaching modules
  • Accompanied by a guide on atomic structure and configurations
