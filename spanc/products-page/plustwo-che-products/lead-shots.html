Lead Shots

High-density metallic spheres

Lead Shots are small, dense metallic spheres composed of pure lead. These are commonly used in laboratory experiments to demonstrate principles of mass, inertia, and motion. They are a valuable tool in educational setups for physics demonstrations across schools and colleges.

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Pure metallic lead
  • Form: Small spherical shots
  • Weight: Packet of 250 grams
  • Storage: Sealed polybag or bottle to prevent contamination

Applications

  • Used in physics experiments for inertia, mass, and motion
  • Ideal for school, college, and laboratory demonstrations

Safety Information

  • Handle with care
  • Avoid ingestion or prolonged contact due to lead content
  • Wash hands thoroughly after handling
