Structure of Atom

Educational model demonstrating the structure of an atom, including atomic orbitals and subatomic particles.

The Structure of Atom model is designed to help visualize the fundamental components of an atom, including the nucleus and orbitals. This model serves as an educational tool for understanding atomic theory and the behavior of protons, neutrons, and electrons in different energy levels.
