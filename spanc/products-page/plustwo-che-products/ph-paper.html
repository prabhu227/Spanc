pH Paper

Reliable indicator for determining pH values in laboratory solutions

The pH Paper is a practical and efficient tool for detecting the acidity or alkalinity of solutions through a clear color change. It is widely used in laboratories, classrooms, and industrial testing environments for quick and accurate pH assessment.

Technical Specifications

  • Type: pH Indicator Paper
  • Range: Broad range (typically pH 1 to 14)
  • Form: Leaf Books
  • Quantity: Each packet contains 10 books
  • Leaves Per Book: 50 leaves
  • Usage: Indicates acidity or alkalinity through color shift
  • Application: Suitable for academic, research, and industrial laboratories
  • Storage: Store in a cool, dry environment
