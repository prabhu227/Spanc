Copper Turning

A versatile laboratory material used in chemical and industrial applications, available in various shapes and sizes.

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Copper (Cu)
  • Form: Turning (Small shavings or filings)
  • Size: Available in various grades and sizes
  • Color: Reddish-brown, typical of copper
  • Purity: 99% pure Copper
  • Uses: Used for metalworking, soldering, and as a conductive material in various experiments
  • Packaging: Typically sold in small packets or jars
  • Applications: Often used for electrical conductivity tests, chemical reactions, and thermodynamic experiments
  • Safety Note: Handle with care to avoid injury from small, sharp filings
