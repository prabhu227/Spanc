Crucible Tong

Essential Tool for Safe Handling of Hot Crucibles

The Crucible Tong is an essential laboratory tool designed for securely handling hot crucibles during heating processes. Made from durable materials, it ensures safe and efficient operation in chemistry labs.

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Iron (Chrome Plated)
  • Sizes Available: 6 inch, 8 inch
  • Grip: Serrated tip for better hold
  • Handle: Insulated for enhanced heat protection
  • Application: Suitable for handling hot crucibles in labs

Additional Details

  • Educational Level: Ideal for high school and college chemistry students
  • Mounting: Easy to place on laboratory tables for demonstrations
  • Learning Outcome: Understanding of laboratory apparatus and safe handling techniques

Applications and Experiment Usage

  • Used to safely handle hot crucibles during heating in laboratory experiments.
  • Ensures stability and safety while performing heating processes in chemistry labs.
