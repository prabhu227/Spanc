Heating Mental (HCS-123)

Product Code: HCS-123

The Heating Mental (HCS-123) is crafted from high-quality glass yarn and designed for safe and consistent heating in laboratory environments. Equipped with an energy regulator and on/off switch, it is ideal for precise heating applications.

Technical Specifications

  • Controls: Energy Regulator and On/Off Switch
  • Voltage: Operates on 220V AC

Applications and Experiment Usage

  • Used for uniform heating of flasks and beakers in chemistry labs
  • Ideal for schools, colleges, and research institutions
