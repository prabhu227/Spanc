Diamond Crystal Model (Open Model)

Product Code: 2111

The Diamond Crystal Model (Open Model) is an educational tool designed to demonstrate the open lattice structure of diamond. This model showcases the unique arrangement of carbon atoms in a three-dimensional, face-centered cubic (FCC) structure, which is fundamental to the properties of diamond.

Technical Specifications

  • Product Code: 2111
  • Model Type: Diamond Crystal Lattice (Open Model)
  • Material: High-quality acrylic or plastic components with metal connectors
  • Lattice Type: Face-Centered Cubic (FCC)
  • Atomic Number: 6
  • Crystal Structure: Each carbon atom is tetrahedrally bonded to four other carbon atoms, creating a 3D open structure
  • Size: Approximately 12-15 inches in diameter
  • Color: Clear/Transparent with color-coded bonds for easy identification
  • Educational Use: Helps visualize the arrangement of carbon atoms in diamond and the nature of covalent bonds in crystals
  • Application: Ideal for classrooms, laboratory demonstrations, and educational workshops
  • Features: Open model allows clear visibility of the bond angles and lattice arrangement
  • Additional Information: Can be used to demonstrate properties of diamond such as hardness, optical clarity, and electrical insulating nature
  • Instructions: Accompanied by a detailed guide explaining the crystal structure and physical properties of diamond

Additional Details

  • Educational Level: Ideal for high school and college chemistry students
  • Mounting: Easily placed on laboratory tables for visual demonstration
  • Learning Outcome: Understanding of molecular structure and the unique properties of diamond

Applications and Experiment Usage

  • Used to demonstrate the three-dimensional arrangement of carbon atoms in diamond crystals.
  • Helps visualize the nature of covalent bonding and its impact on material properties.
