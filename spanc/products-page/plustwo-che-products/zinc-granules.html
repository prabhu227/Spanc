Zinc Granules

A versatile material used in a variety of chemical reactions and laboratory experiments, available in granule form for easy handling.

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Zinc (Zn)
  • Form: Granules
  • Size: Available in various granule sizes
  • Color: Silvery-white, metallic appearance
  • Purity: 99.9% pure Zinc
  • Packaging: Typically sold in 500g packets or small jars
  • Applications: Used in reduction reactions, electroplating, and as a catalyst in certain chemical reactions
  • Safety Note: Handle with care, as zinc granules can react with acids and water to release hydrogen gas
