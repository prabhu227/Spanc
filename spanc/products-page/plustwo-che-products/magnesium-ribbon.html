Magnesium Metal Ribbon

A high-quality laboratory material used in various chemical reactions and experiments.

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Magnesium (Mg)
  • Form: Ribbon (Flexible strip)
  • Length: Available in various lengths (customizable)
  • Width: Standard 1-5 mm
  • Thickness: Thin, consistent strip for easy handling
  • Purity: 99% pure Magnesium
  • Color: Silvery-white
  • Uses: Commonly used in combustion reactions, as a reducing agent, and for thermite reactions
  • Packaging: Available in rolls or pre-cut lengths
  • Safety Note: Handle with care, as magnesium burns with intense white light when ignited
