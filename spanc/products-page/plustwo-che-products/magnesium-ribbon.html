Magnesium Metal Ribbon

A high-quality laboratory material for chemical reactions and demonstrations

Magnesium Metal Ribbon is a flexible, silvery-white strip of pure magnesium used in educational and research laboratories. It is often used in combustion demonstrations, chemical reactions, and thermite-based experiments due to its high reactivity and ease of ignition.

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Magnesium (Mg)
  • Form: Ribbon (Flexible strip)
  • Length: Customizable
  • Width: Standard 1–5 mm
  • Thickness: Thin, consistent strip for easy handling
  • Purity: 99% pure Magnesium
  • Color: Silvery-white
  • Packaging: Rolls or pre-cut lengths

Applications

  • Used in combustion reactions and thermite demonstrations
  • Acts as a reducing agent in chemical experiments
  • Ideal for science education and lab training

Safety Note

Handle with care—magnesium burns with an intense white light and should be used in a well-ventilated area with proper eye protection.
