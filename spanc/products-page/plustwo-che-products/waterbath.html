Water Bath

Durable copper concentric ring water bath for laboratory heating applications

The Water Bath is designed for laboratory use with a durable copper construction and concentric ring design. It is ideal for heating samples with precision, providing a uniform temperature distribution.

Technical Specifications

  • Material: Copper, die-pressed and polished
  • Design: Hemispherical shape with side handles
  • Top: Concentric rings for variable flask/tube sizes
  • Available Sizes:
    • Size A: 4"
    • Size B: 5"
    • Size C: 6"
    • Size D: 8"
  • Application: Ideal for heating samples uniformly
