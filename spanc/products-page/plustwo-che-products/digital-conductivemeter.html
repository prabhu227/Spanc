Digital Conductivity/TDS Meter (AI-106/107)

Product Code: AI-106/107

The Digital Conductivity/TDS Meter (AI-106/107) is a microprocessor-based device designed for precise measurement of TDS (Total Dissolved Solids) and EC (Electrical Conductivity) in laboratory and educational settings. It features automatic ranging and temperature compensation, making it ideal for use in diverse laboratory applications.

Technical Specifications

  • Product Code: AI-106/107
  • Type: Microprocessor-Based
  • Display: 16 x 2 Alphanumeric
  • Range: TDS 0 to 1000 ppt, EC 0 to 1000 µS (Auto Ranging)
  • Resolution: TDS 0.1 ppm, EC 0.1 µS
  • Accuracy: ± 0.5% Range
  • Cell Constant: Adjustable
  • Measuring Cell: Platinum DP Type
  • Temperature Compensation: Manual
  • Dimension: 275 x 187 x 94 mm (approx)
  • Power: 230 V ±10V AC, 50 Hz
  • Accessories: Conductivity Cell, Cell Stand, and Operating Manual

Additional Details

  • Educational Level: Suitable for high school and college labs
  • Mounting: Designed for easy tabletop placement
  • Learning Outcome: Understanding water quality parameters and conductivity measurement

Applications and Experiment Usage

  • Used to measure the conductivity and TDS of water in laboratory experiments.
  • Ensures accurate and reliable measurements for water quality analysis.
  • Ideal for educational settings to demonstrate principles of conductivity and TDS measurement.
