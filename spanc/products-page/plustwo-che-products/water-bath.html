Water Bath Rectangular (HCS-111)

Stainless steel water bath designed for precise control of liquid temperatures in laboratories

The Water Bath Rectangular (HCS-111) is ideal for laboratory applications that require precise temperature control. Available in two sizes, it can accommodate 6 or 12 holes, providing flexibility for various experiments.

Technical Specifications

  • Size A: 300×250×100 mm with 6 holes (75 mm diameter)
  • Size B: 355×405×100 mm with 12 holes (75 mm diameter)
  • Capacity: 8 Liters (A), 15 Liters (B)
