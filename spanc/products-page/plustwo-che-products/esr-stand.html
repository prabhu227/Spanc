ESR Stand

Product Code: 2065

The ESR Stand is a reliable laboratory tool designed for securely holding capillary tubes during Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate (ESR) testing. Constructed with a strong base, this stand provides stability during testing and ensures accurate results.

Technical Specifications

  • Product Code: 2065
  • Material: Iron, Copper Plated (C.P.)
  • Tube Holding Capacity: Available in 3 and 6 tube options
  • Design: Vertical tube support with stable base
  • Application: Suitable for ESR (Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate) tests
  • Finish: Smooth and corrosion-resistant plating

Additional Details

  • Educational Level: Ideal for use in medical and laboratory environments
  • Mounting: Provides a stable setup for precise ESR testing

Applications and Experiment Usage

  • Used for securing capillary tubes during ESR testing in medical and laboratory environments.
  • Ensures stable and accurate measurements in the determination of sedimentation rates.
